The high intelligent spatial computing team visited SuperMap Group to visit and study

access_time August 14, 2023
by   Dachuan XU

In order to further enhance team members' understanding of the prospects of the geographic information industry, on the afternoon of August 14, researcher Wang Shaohua led all members of the highly intelligent spatial computing team to visit SuperMap Group for study. During this exchange and study, we had an in-depth understanding of the development history of SuperMap Group, the latest progress of the Innovation Research Institute, and the group’s endorsement of activities on university campuses, and jointly witnessed the brilliant achievements of SuperMap Group’s technological innovation.


First, SuperMap Group introduced us to its impressive development history. Over the years, SuperMap has been committed to the research and application of geographic information technology, constantly promoting the progress of the geographic information industry. The development history of the Group fully demonstrates its solid foundation in the field of technology and its spirit of continuous innovation.


The Innovation Research Institute is the technological innovation engine of SuperMap Group. In the introduction, we learned about the current situation of the Innovation Institute and their cutting-edge research results in different fields, which will provide strong support for future scientific and technological development.

Kaineng Xiao, director of the Government Business Department of SuperMap Group, introduced in detail the group’s latest progress in technological innovation. She emphasized the group's breakthroughs in technology research and development and application. Lu Wang, manager of the Education Ecology Department, introduced to us a series of competition endorsement activities organized by SuperMap Group on university campuses to encourage young people to actively participate in the innovation and application of geographic information technology.


TutuHui's general manager briefly introduced the company's mission and business scope to us, emphasizing TutuHui's innovation and influence in the field of geographic information.

Finally, researcher Shaohua Wang gave a wonderful report on behalf of the team. He shared the team’s latest research results and project progress, highlighting SuperMap Group’s leading position in technological innovation.


This exchange and learning activity not only enhanced our understanding of SuperMap Group, but also demonstrated its huge potential in the field of technological innovation. We look forward to continuing to work closely with SuperMap Group in the future to jointly create a new future for geographic information technology.
