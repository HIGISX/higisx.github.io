Shaohua Wang’s team at the Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) is recruiting visiting graduate students

access_time September 18, 2023
by   Shaohua Wang

Shaohua Wang’s team of innovative researchers from the Key Laboratory of Digital Earth of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the International Research Center for Big Data for Sustainable Development is now recruiting 2-3 visiting master’s/doctoral students to meet the needs of scientific research work. The research directions include: geospatial analysis, remote sensing Algorithms and applications, geographical big data analysis, geospatial intelligence and spatial optimization, etc.

I. Application Requirements:

1.Master's or doctoral students currently studying or within 2 years of graduation (for current students, completion of compulsory courses is required with a minimum duration of one and a half years for the visiting research period).

2.Physically and mentally healthy, with good communication skills, independent thinking ability, and a sense of responsibility towards work.

3.Proficiency in computer programming and system development, familiar with Python or other programming languages; or strong application skills in software such as SuperMap/QGIS/ArcGIS/ENVI. Priority will be given to applicants with GIS development or GeoAI project experience.

4.Good proficiency in written and reading skills in Chinese and English (prior publications in Chinese or English journals are preferred).

II. Benefits:

1.Support to complete the graduate thesis within the research group, assist in publishing SCI papers or high-quality Chinese journal papers, apply for software copyrights, patents, and provide academic exchange opportunities.

2.Refer to the graduate student benefits of the CAS AAIR. Outstanding performance may be rewarded with additional research incentives, which can be discussed further.

III. Job Content and Method:

1.Choose research topics based on your own interests, such as geographic spatial analysis, remote sensing and GIS application and development, data-driven, model-driven, or data-model coupling-driven GeoAI algorithm research, etc.

2.Other related tasks in the research group.

IV. Application Process:

1.Applicants should submit an application letter via email (please indicate "Visiting Graduate Student Application - Your Name" in the subject line), along with a personal resume, representative achievements or code, and other relevant materials to demonstrate their abilities.

2.Communication will be conducted via email or phone, with interviews conducted for pre-selected qualified candidates, and applicants will be notified to provide a consent letter from their current school or research institute advisor.

3.Deadline for application: Until positions are filled.

V. Contact Information:

Contact Person: Shaohua Wang

Email: wangshaohua@aircas.ac.cn
