Juan Wang

Juan Wang
Beijing Union University
Associate Professor

Biography

Wang Juan, female, born in 1986, Zhoukou, Henan Province, is an associate professor and a master's advisor in the Department of Urban Science, College of Applied Arts and Sciences, Beijing Union University.

In 2014, She obtained a Doctor of Science degree in the Department of Geography and Resource Management at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. Subsequently conducted postdoctoral research at the Institute of Geographic Sciences and Natural Resources Research, Chinese Academy of Sciences. She also served as a member of the Surveying and Mapping Society and was recognized as a young outstanding talent in Beijing.

Research interests

  1. Geologic analysis of remotely sensed images
  2. Intelligent extraction of remote sensing information
  3. Data mining and knowledge discovery based on textual big data
  4. Integrating geo-big data in urban environmental applications
