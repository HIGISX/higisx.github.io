Yuke Zhou

Yuke Zhou
Institute of Geographic Sciences and Natural Resources Research,
Chinese Academy of Sciences
Associate Research Fellow

Biography

Yuke Zhou, male, born in 1984, Jining, Shandong Province, PhD, is an associate researcher at Institute of Geographic Sciences and Natural Resources Research,CAS, and is a master's supervisor..

He is mainly engaged in applied research on remote sensing, GIS and spatial and temporal big data in ecology, such as remote sensing monitoring of vegetation and climate change and ecological effects, urban change based on remote sensing of nighttime lighting, and so on.

He has the qualification of Information System Project Manager (Senior) from the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. As the subject leader and backbone, he has undertaken and participated in many projects of National Key R&D Program, National Natural Science Foundation of China, Youth Foundation, Strategic Pilot Science and Technology Special Project of Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), and STS project of CAS.

A total of 46 papers have been published (2015-present), of which 37 are first or corresponding authors, including:
· 12 SCI-indexed articles, of which 9 are first or corresponding authors
· 25 Chinese core papers (of which 21 were first/corresponding authors) and 9 other papers
· Participated in the editing of one monograph
· We have obtained 9 national invention patents and more than 20 computer software copyrights.

Research interests

  1. ecological remote sensing
  2. Spatiotemporal Big Data Mining
