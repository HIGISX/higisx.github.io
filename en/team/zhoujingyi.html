Jingyi Zhou

Jingyi Zhou
Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences
Special Research Assistant

Biography

Dr. Jingyi Zhou is a Special Research Assistant at the Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Her primary research areas include optimization of sustainable urban service facility allocation, urban spatiotemporal big data analysis, and indoor navigation map design.

Research interests

  1. Optimization of sustainable urban service facility allocation
  2. Urban spatiotemporal big data analysis
  3. Indoor navigation map design

Education

  • 2019.09-2025.09, Nanjing Normal University, School of Geography, Cartography and Geography information system, Joined Study of Master and Doctoral Program, Ph.D.

  • 2023.01-2023.12, University of Zurich, Department of Geography, Joint Ph.D. Studying Program (funded by CSC)

  • 2015.06-2019.09, Jiangxi Normal University, School of Geography and Environment, Geography Information Science, B.S.

Experience

  • 2025.10-Present, Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Special Research Assistant
