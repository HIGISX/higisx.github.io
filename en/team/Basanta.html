Basanta Kumar Neupane

Basanta Kumar Neupane
Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences

Biography

Basanta Neupane is a geographer interested in spatial study, spatial technology, health geography, and geospatial planning. He holds a master's in Geography from the Central Department of Geography, Tribhuvan University. He is pursuing a PhD from the Chinese Academy of Sciences specializing in Cartography and GIS. Mr. Neupane has experience in the geospatial field, working in various academic, government, and non-government organizations as a GIS expert in Nepal.

Education

  • 2020.07—Present, Aerospace Information Research Institute, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Cartography and GIS, Ph.D.

  • 2017.05—2019.06, Central Department of Geography, Tribhuvan University, Geography, Master's degree

Research interests

  1. Environmental Health
  2. Spatial assessment
  3. Modelling and mapping of lung cancer in Nepal
  4. Assess spatial inequalities in health care services in the context of SDGs.
