Biography

Xinwei Yang, Male, Han nationality, PhD, is an assistant research fellow at the Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Research interests

  1. SAR Tomograhpy
  2. Polarimetric SAR Interferometry
  3. Forestry Quantitative Remote Sensing

Education

  • 2015-09-2020-06, Wuhan University, Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing, Joined Study of Master and Doctoral Degree

  • 2009-09-2013-06, Wuhan University, Geographic Information System, B.S.

Experience

  • 2020-07-Present, Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Assistant Research Fellow
