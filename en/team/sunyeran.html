Yeran Sun

Yeran Sun
University of Lincoln
Senior Lecturer in Geography

Biography

Dr Yeran Sun is a Senior Lecturer in Geography since 2022. Prior to joining University of Lincoln, he was a Lecturer at Swansea University (2020-2022) and a Research Associate at University of Glasgow (2016-2019).

His research fields include Urban Informatics, Urban Remote Sensing, Urban Transport, Health Geography, and Big Data Analytics. These studies have been published in journals such as Environment and Planning B, Computers Environment and Urban Systems, Sustainable Cities and Society, Journal of Transport & Health, and International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health, and in ‘Complex Networks and Dynamic Systems: Transportation Analytics in the Era of Big Data’ as book chapters.

Research interests

  1. Transport and Mobility
  2. Healthy Built Environment
  3. Geospatial Big Data Analytics
  4. GeoAI

Education
